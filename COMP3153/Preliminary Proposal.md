## Ideas
### Buggy parser for simplified regex
Subset includes:
- `[characters]` - group
- `character` - character
- `(group|character)?` - 0 or 1 of
- `(group|character)+` - 1 or more of
- `.`
- `[^characters]` - beginning
- `$` - end
- any ascii character
- escape special characters with `\`
